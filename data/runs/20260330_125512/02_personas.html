Persona Profiles

Design Rationale

For a 100-person SaaS startup, employee satisfaction drivers vary significantly by tenure, role seniority, and growth stage expectations. Two personas are optimal here: (1) an early-stage believer invested in the mission who frames engagement through autonomy and impact—representing the core early hires who drive culture—and (2) a recent joiner with external reference points who experiences the startup through operational friction and career trajectory clarity—representing growth-phase hires who evaluate satisfaction pragmatically. This split captures the tension between mission-driven engagement (which declines as startups scale) and structural satisfaction (which becomes critical as processes formalize). Together they surface both what TestCorp is doing right culturally and where scaling creates dissatisfaction, giving the VP HR actionable insights on retention risk and engagement levers.
