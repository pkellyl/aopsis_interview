Persona Profiles

Design Rationale

This research requires representation across the full breadth of the finance function at a single-site gold mine, capturing both operational embedding and central transactional processing. Five personas are necessary and sufficient to cover: (1) the finance leadership and strategic layer, (2) the on-site business partnering model embedded in operations, (3) the transactional/processing core (AP, AR, payroll), (4) the systems and reporting backbone, and (5) a newer team member whose fresher perspective surfaces undocumented processes and inefficiencies. This mix captures the site-vs-central tension, tenure diversity, and the full span of processes from procurement through reporting. The personas represent different vantage points on the same processes, enabling cross-validation and complete process documentation. We exclude corporate finance and focus entirely on the operational and site-level finance function.
