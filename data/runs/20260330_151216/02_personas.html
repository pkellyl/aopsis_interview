Persona Profiles

Design Rationale

For a focused diagnostic of decision-making topology in a 350-person supply chain function, 5 personas capture the essential decision-making layers and cross-functional friction points without redundancy. We selected: (1) the VP to understand apex strategy and governance architecture, (2) a Director from Procurement (the largest spend and most complex category) to represent strategic sourcing decisions, (3) a Director from Production Planning to capture the critical intersection between demand, supply, and manufacturing, (4) a Senior Manager from Procurement to represent day-to-day tactical sourcing and the manager-level decision boundary, and (5) a Plant Supply Chain Manager to represent how central decisions execute locally and where disconnects emerge. This covers vertical decision authority (executive to manager to specialist), horizontal cross-function dynamics (especially the supply-demand interface), and the critical seam between central strategy and plant-floor reality. Together they expose decision clarity, KPI alignment, meeting effectiveness, and escalation pathways across the function's most consequential domains.
